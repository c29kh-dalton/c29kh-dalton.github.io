The purpose of our project is to measure the
temperatures in Mott Haven and the Upper
West Side, which are New York City
neighborhoods. We will look at the average
temperatures in these neighborhoods during
a hot summer day, explain why they would
experience different average temperatures,
and provide solutions to decrease high
temperatures. Additionally, we will be
exploring vegetative covers and heat
vulnerability index throughout both
neighborhoods.
